Okonomiyaki

Okonomiyaki is a dish from the Kansai region of Japan.

It is typically made from cabbage and batter, and typically incorporates fried pork (though other ingredients are possible; okonomiyaki literally means "fried as you like it").

Hiroshima-style okonomiyaki adds noodles to the mix.

Ingredients

  • Cabbage
  • Batter
  • Sliced pork
  • Mayonnaise
  • Okonomiyaki sauce
  • (Optional) yakisoba noodles
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Prepare batter
  2. Chop the cabbage
  3. Slice pork
  4. Heat oil in a large frying pan or on a teppan grill
  5. Mix batter, cabbage, and sliced pork and apply to frying pan
  6. Scuplt into disc and cook until one side is light brown
  7. Flip and cook other side until brown
  8. Turn off heat and add mayonnaise and okonomiyaki sauce to your liking
