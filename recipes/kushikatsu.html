Kushikatsu

Kushikatsu, the Japanese shish kabob

Enjoy the meat, vegetable, or even cheese of your choice skewerd on a pointy stick and deepfried.

Another favorite from the Kansai region, kushikatsu can give you a real taste of South Osaka.

Ingredients

  • Beef cut or
  • Pork cut
  • Chicken cut or
  • Any kind of vegetable or
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Deepfry until not raw but not totally burned either
  2. Serve on grated metal plate
  3. Apply tonkatsu sauce to your liking
